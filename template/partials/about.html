About Smart Garden

The recommended Smart Garden system automatically manages and monitors maintenance schedules for different kinds of plants. The system enables on spot and on the go (Android Mobile Application with Wi-Fi) monitoring strategy with the use of latest up-to- date technology which will improve the efficiency and accuracy of the overall system in terms of information and data gathered. Some of the ways that this system achieves its desired results are:

1- Sensing and displaying the water level from the source.
2- Controlling water level/flow based on specific quantity from the source based on the soil moisture level and temperature.
3- Sensing and displaying the moisture of the soil.
4- Sensing and displaying the temperature of the surrounding.
5- Turning plant grow light on/off depending on when it is dark or there is sunlight and displaying itsâ€™ status as well.
6- Displaying current timing and next scheduled maintenance event for the plants. 7- Providing the functionality to select the right plant category.
8- Editing the setting or time schedule for next scheduled maintenance event for the plants.
9- Providing alerts/notifications on the system for scheduled events and incorrect settings.
10- Providing default optimum maintenance settings for the chosen plant category.
11- Providing a camera-based functionality to perform a simple plant health analysis from the data gathered by sensors.
